About VoidRecon

Sub‑1 GHz Swiss Army knife – Sniff, record, replay, and brute‑force signals with an ESP32 and a CC1101 transceiver. Wide frequency coverage (300‑928 MHz), multiple modulations (2‑FSK, GFSK, ASK/OOK, MSK), adjustable data rate, packet engine, real‑time sniffer, signal analysis, recording & playback, jamming, brute‑force, and chat mode.

Flasher

📐 Building Guide

PCB version: Use the official PCB design (Gerber files in PCB/). Solder the Atmega32u4, CC1101 module, antenna, and passives. Flash the firmware via USB.

Breadboard (ESP32): Connect CC1101 pins to ESP32: VCC→3.3V, GND→GND, SCLK→GPIO18, SO(MISO)→GPIO19, SI(MOSI)→GPIO23, CSn→GPIO5, GDO2→GPIO4 (optional), GDO0→GPIO2 (optional). Attach a quarter‑wave antenna (~17.3 cm for 433 MHz).

Firmware: Flash voidrecon.bin via esptool.py or use the Python CLI. Connect at 115200 baud.
